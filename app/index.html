FAQ

How can I book a videographer with Splento?

If you're looking for a videographer in London, you can book one directly on our website. For the bookings in other cities - we now operate in all major cities WORLDWIDE - please contact our customer support at splento@splento.com or call 020 8123 1838.

When will I receive my videos? How long does it take you to deliver the video?

You will receive the first version of the video within 48 hours after your event. After that, you will have an opportunity to request changes to this draft. Please notice, that 2 version changes are included into the price, any extra change requests will have an extra cost.

In what format will I receive the final videos? How is our video sent to us?

After we've edited your video, you will receive a link with all the videos you requested. The standard format is 1920x1080 (full hd) .mp4, which can be used on the majority of the social media platforms.

Can I hire multiple videographers?

Yes, you can hire as many videographers as you need for your event. For big events and conferences we recommend booking at least two videographers, which will help you capture the most important moments of your event from multiple angles.

How far in advance should I book the videographer?

Depending on the location of the videography session, you can book a videographer in as little as 4 hours in advance. Please contact our customer support team to find out the details at splento@splento.com or call 020 8123 1838.

How long will the video be?

You can select the length of the video based on your needs. We have tailored pricing for different types of videos. Please contact our customer support team to find out the details at splento@splento.com or call 020 8123 1838.

Can we have a custom videogrphy package?

Yes, we can make the necessary arrangements to tailor the package specifically to your needs. Please email us at splento@splento.com or call 020 8123 1838.

Do you use drones for recording your videos?

Yes, depending on your needs, we can provide you with the licensed drone operator. Please contact our customer support team to find out the details at splento@splento.com or call 020 8123 1838.

Filming

We offer a broad range of filming options for our clients. This starts at a single operator with a digital camcorder and can go all the way through to a fully crewed shoot on 35mm film. We make sure our productions are tailored to meet clients requirements and their budgets without sacrificing quality.

We have a great deal of equipment in house which enables us to move quickly on projects as well as to create extremely flexible shooting schedules but if you have bespoke needs then we are able to broker in specialist crew and kit for productions.
